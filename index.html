  1. (a-b)2=a2-2ab+b2
  2. (a+b)2=a2+2ab+b2
  3. (a-b)3=a3-3a2b+3ab2-b2
  4. (a+b)3=a3+3a2b+3ab2+b2
  5. (a-b)4=a4-4a3b+6a2b2-4ab3+b4
  6. (a+b)4=a4+4a3b+6a2b2+4ab3+b4

  7. Binominal Formula

    (a+b)n=nC0an+nC1an-1b+nC2an-2b2
    +...+nCn-1abn-1+nCnbn,
    where nCk=n! / k!(n-k)! are the binominal coefficients.
  8. (a+b+c)2=a2+b2+c2+2ab+2ac+2bc
  9. (a+b+c+...+u+v)2=a2+b2+c2+...+u2+v2
    +2(ab+ac+...+au+av+bc+...+bu+bv+...+uv)

  10. (a)2+(b)2=(a+b)2+2ab
  11. (a)2-(b)2=(a+b)(a-b)
  12. (a)3-(b)3=(a-b)(a2+ab+b2)
  13. (a)3+(b)3=(a+b)(a2-ab+b2)
  14. (a)4-(b)4=(a2-b2)(a2+b2)
  15. (a)4-(b)4=(a-b)(a4+a3b+a2b2+ab3+b4)
  16. (a)4+(b)4=(a+b)(a4-a3b+a2b2-ab3+b4)

  17. if n is odd,then

    (a)n+(b)n=(a+b)(an-1+an-2b+an-3b2-...-abn-2+bn-1).

  18. if n is even,then

    (a)n-(b)n=(a-b)(an-1+an-2b+an-3b2+...+abn-2+bn-1),
    (a)n+(b)n=(a+b)(an-1-an-2b+an-3b2-...+abn-2-bn-1).
